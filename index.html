Middle school section

    Tips and Rules before posting a picture or a comment


    1.BE RESPECTFUL

    2.Have Fun

    3.NOONE IS PERFECT

    4.KEEP YOUR Private Information to Yourself

    5.Don't be rude

    6.Dont spam on someone else's page

    7.Write comments that will help others or that will be supportive

    8.Do not give away your PERSONAL INFORMATION TO STRANGERS!

    9.If you see any suspecious picture or a very DISRESPCTFUL comment or story REPORT IT!
